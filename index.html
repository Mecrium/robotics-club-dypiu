About the Club

Our History

The Robotics Club was established under the Semiconductor Engineering Department of DY Patil International University. Initially managed by a single dedicated senior student, the club has now evolved under the second committee leadership.

Our Mission

To foster innovation and technical excellence by providing a platform for students to design, build, and test robotic solutions. We aim to create engineers who can solve real-world problems through robotics.

Our Vision

To become the leading robotics club, recognized for groundbreaking projects, competitive excellence, and fostering a culture of innovation and collaboration.

Our Goals

Organize quarterly workshops on advanced robotics topics. Participate in national competitions. Develop innovative projects in AI, automation, and embedded systems.
